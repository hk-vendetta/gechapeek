GETCHA - CHAPTER 1 STORY DRAFT
Working title: Ashes of Oath

Purpose
- Establish Aric the Bold as the central protagonist.
- Build trust in The Lord Marshall as a wise ruler and father figure.
- Form a lovable core party through shared hardship, humor, and sacrifice.
- End Chapter 1 with a major betrayal and cliffhanger that points into Chapter 2.

Story Pillars (Chapter 1)
- Emotion: brotherhood, found family, grief, and hope after loss.
- Deceit: The Lord Marshall appears noble while manipulating events in secret.
- Unity: unlikely allies from different paths choose to stand together.
- Cost of duty: loyalty to crown versus loyalty to truth.

Main Chapter 1 Cast (Playable)
- Aric the Bold (unit_001): decorated general, disciplined, honorable.
- Lyria (unit_002): starlight mage, warm humor, emotional heart of the team.
- Kain (unit_003): shadow assassin, sarcastic, distrustful, learns to trust.
- Sera (unit_004): holy sentinel, moral compass, protective and principled.
- Zephyr (unit_005): storm caller, comic relief, surprising depth.
- Draven (unit_006): iron warlord, blunt veteran, loyalty tested.
- Mirel (unit_007): tide priestess, empathetic healer, voice of grief and healing.
- Vex (unit_008): cursed blade, feared outsider, key to late Chapter 1 turn.

Reserved Primarily for Chapter 2 (Playable)
- Codename Ember Warden (unit_011)
- Codename Vale Seer (unit_012)
Notes:
- They can be teased in Chapter 1 via rumors, letters, or silhouette cameos, but not fully recruited yet.

Key Non-Playable Characters (Chapter 1)
- The Lord Marshall: ruler of the kingdom, Aric's mentor, public hero, hidden architect of chaos.
- Captain Elowen (new NPC): command staff liaison, practical and loyal to Aric.
- Brother Thane (new NPC): archivist-priest who discovers forbidden records.
- Twinfang Chief (existing enemy title): used as visible local antagonist before the true villain reveal.

Playable Availability Plan
- Start Available: Aric, Lyria.
- Unlocks through Chapter 1 progression:
  - Kain at Dungeon 1 finale.
  - Sera at Dungeon 2 midpoint.
  - Zephyr at Dungeon 2 finale.
  - Draven at Dungeon 3 midpoint.
  - Mirel at Dungeon 4 midpoint.
  - Vex at Chapter 1 final act (late, high-impact reveal ally).

CHAPTER 1 STRUCTURE
- Part I: The Green Oath (Dungeons 1-2)
- Part II: Embers of Command (Dungeons 3-4)
- Chapter Finale: The Furnace of Truth (Mission 012 and ending cutscene)

PART I - THE GREEN OATH
Dungeons covered:
- Dungeon 001: Verdant Clearing (Missions 001-003)
- Dungeon 002: Deeproot Hollow (Missions 004-006)

Opening Cinematic
- Ceremonial scene in the capital.
- The Lord Marshall publicly praises Aric as "the shield of the realm."
- Lyria jokes that Aric only smiles during battles and funerals; Aric cracks a rare smile.
- Disturbance report arrives: attacks near Whispering Woods.
- The Lord Marshall calmly orders Aric to resolve it quickly and quietly.

Mission Beat Mapping
- Mission 001 - Into the Clearing:
  - Tone: introduction and chemistry.
  - Party banter: Lyria teases Aric's seriousness; Aric counters with dry humor.
- Mission 002 - The Pack's Cry:
  - First emotional warning: villagers mention missing scouts and strange symbols.
- Mission 003 - Edge of the Woods:
  - Reveal Kain as a masked observer tracking the same threat.
  - Kain reluctantly aids the fight, then challenges Aric's trust in the crown.
  - Mild part cliffhanger setup: "Someone is funding these raids."

Transition to Deeproot Hollow
- Captain Elowen reports multiple coordinated attacks, too disciplined for random bandits.
- The Lord Marshall sends praise and a private seal granting Aric expanded authority.
- Lyria is impressed; Kain is suspicious.

- Mission 004 - Mushroom Labyrinth:
  - Introduce Sera, tending wounded civilians and refusing to abandon them.
  - Sera joins temporarily for escort.
- Mission 005 - Root Warrens:
  - Zephyr enters in a chaotic rescue with storm magic and jokes to break tension.
  - Comedic beat: Zephyr and Kain argue over "stealth" versus "style."
- Mission 006 - Hollow's Heart:
  - Swamp shaman defeated; clues indicate orders signed with a royal cipher.
  - Brother Thane secretly confirms the cipher should be impossible to forge.

Part I Mild Cliffhanger
- Back at camp, Aric receives a personal letter from The Lord Marshall: "Trust no one beyond your command."
- Kain notices the wax seal is fresh, but the parchment smells of volcanic ash, not the capital.
- Tease for Part II: all roads point toward Ashfield Pass.

PART II - EMBERS OF COMMAND
Dungeons covered:
- Dungeon 003: Ashfield Pass (Missions 007-009)
- Dungeon 004: Cinder Keep (Missions 010-012)

Part II Opening
- The team crosses into Ember Foothills.
- Tone shifts from woodland mystery to military escalation.
- Aric struggles between duty to The Lord Marshall and loyalty to his growing team.

Mission Beat Mapping
- Mission 007 - Smoldering Trail:
  - Draven appears leading a battered holdout unit.
  - Draven and Aric share history from prior campaigns.
  - Emotional beat: Draven lost most of his squad protecting civilians.
- Mission 008 - Bandit Crossing:
  - Team conflict: Kain accuses command of setting a trap.
  - Lyria diffuses tension with humor, then privately admits fear to Aric.
- Mission 009 - Scorched Watchtower:
  - Twinfang Chief vanguard defeated.
  - Discovery: supply crates stamped with a secret armory mark tied to royal command.

Approach to Cinder Keep
- Mirel joins after saving survivors from volcanic collapse.
- She brings testimony: "soldiers" burned villages to create panic corridors.
- Sera and Mirel scene on grief and faith: one prays, one listens; both choose action.

- Mission 010 - Iron Gate:
  - Golems and rune sentries suggest old defense systems were reactivated intentionally.
- Mission 011 - The Armory:
  - Team learns the keep is a staging ground, not a rebel fortress.
  - Vex appears as a hostile "cursed enforcer," then spares civilians and vanishes.
- Mission 012 - Furnace Hall:
  - Epic battle sequence against Twinfang Chief and Rage Cyclops.
  - Team victory is costly; Draven critically wounded protecting Aric.

PART II Mild Cliffhanger
- In the quiet after battle, the party finds sealed orders naming Aric's unit as expendable.
- Signature appears to be The Lord Marshall's hand.
- Aric refuses to believe it and insists on returning to the capital for answers.

CHAPTER 1 MAJOR FINALE - THE BETRAYAL OF CINDERS
Final Cutscene Sequence (post Mission 012)
1) Return and Honor
- The Lord Marshall welcomes Aric publicly as a hero.
- He embraces Aric like a son and awards him before the court.
- Player is encouraged to trust him again.

2) The Private Audience
- The Lord Marshall asks Aric to attend a final "cleansing operation" at a hidden chamber below Cinder Keep.
- He claims a greater demon is awakening and only Aric's team can stop it.

3) The Deception
- In the hidden chamber, runes activate and bind the party.
- The Lord Marshall reveals his true goal: he engineered the regional chaos to harvest battle-born aether and open the way to a higher power.
- He praises Aric's loyalty as the final key.

4) Emotional Collapse
- Aric realizes every victory fed the ritual.
- Lyria confronts The Lord Marshall in fury and heartbreak.
- Sera declares him fallen from grace.
- Kain: "I told you kings lie. I just hoped this one didn't."

5) Epic Escape / Loss
- Vex returns and breaks the rune lock at personal cost, enabling the party to flee.
- Chamber collapses; unclear if Vex survives.
- The Lord Marshall remains inside the firelight, calm, and smiling.

6) Chapter 1 Final Cliffhanger (To Be Continued)
- Sky tears with a crimson fracture above the keep.
- The Lord Marshall's voice echoes: "Let the second march begin."
- Final shot: two silhouettes (Codename Ember Warden and Codename Vale Seer tease) watch the fracture from afar.
- Text card: TO BE CONTINUED IN CHAPTER 2 - THE SHATTERED MARCH
