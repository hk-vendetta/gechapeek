# Oathsworn: Compact - Battle Guide (Player v2)

Last updated: 2026-04-08

## Battle Loop
1. Queue actions for your units.
2. Execute queued actions in your chosen order.
3. Enemy phase resolves after your eligible units act.

## Party and Roles
- Up to 6 units in battle.
- Build around damage, support, cleanse, and durability.

## Actions
- Attack
- Skill
- Magic
- Weave (Oath-Weaver only)
- Defend
- Items (if available in battle loadout)

## Targeting
- Tap enemy battlefield sprites directly.
- Indicators show selected target.
- Some actions require explicit target selection.

## Chain and Timing
- Chains scale with real hit timing.
- Multi-hit actions are strong chain starters.
- Action order matters more than raw stats alone.

## Status and Recovery
- Status effects can disable actions.
- Cleansing during your phase can restore a unit before phase end.
- Items and skills can heal, revive, and cure based on config.

## Sigils and Weaves
- Passive resonance bonuses apply from compatible sigil pairings.
- Weaves are mission-limited and should be saved for high-impact turns.

## Practical Tips
1. Queue a plan first, then execute for chain efficiency.
2. Cleanse key units before spending damage turns.
3. Use Weave for pivots: emergency heal/cleanse or burst windows.
4. Keep one stable support unit for long fights.
